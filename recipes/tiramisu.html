Easy tiramisu recipe

A quick and easy tiramisu to satiate your cravings

A classic recipe to satiate your dessert craving

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp instant coffee granules
  • 85g condensed milk
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp coffee liqueur

Steps

  1. Mix granules with boiling water in a large jug and add coffee liqueur> Pur into a dish and set aside
  2. Make cream layer by bating the condensed milk and vanilla extract until thick
  3. Sift over cocoa and chill for 1 hour before serving
