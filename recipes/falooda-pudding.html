Falooda Pudding Recipe

Tasty falooda pudding

A foolproof recipe for the popular asian dessert, falooda pudding. If you're looking for a refreshing dessert for the summer days, then the falooda dessert is a perfect choice. It's freaking delicious, cooling and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Prepare 2 tbsp of Agaragar powder (china grass powder)
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup Nestle cream
  • 12 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/4 tsp red food colouring

Steps

  1. Boil all the ingredients (except essence and cream)
  2. Remove from flame and add essence, cream, and color and whisk. Let it cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour on pudding bowls and sprinkle with cardamom powder

Enjoy the dessert!
